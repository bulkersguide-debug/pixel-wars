⚡ THE BASICS
🗺️
THE GRID
A 2000×2000 pixel canvas divided into 400 sectors. Claim pixels for your fandom. Most pixels at season end wins.
🏴
CLAIM
Select your fandom → BUILD mode → drag across empty pixels. Adjacent pixels cost 10% less — build from your base!
⚔️
RAID
RAID mode lets you steal unshielded enemy pixels. Fortified sectors cost 3×. Requires Discord login.
🏆
WIN
Seasons last ~90 days. The fandom with the most pixels when the timer hits zero becomes Season Champion.

⚡ PRO TIPS
🗺️Build connected territory — adjacent claims cost 10% less. Expand from your base, not randomly.
🌍Save War Chest gold for Faction Raids — stealing 30 enemy-faction pixels at once swings the World War standings dramatically.
⚡Check the Daily Sector Challenge every day — 50% off for 2 hours is the best free value in the game. Set a reminder.
🏰Avoid fortified sectors when raiding — 3× cost. Target edges of enemy territory in regular sectors instead.
⚡Use Surge carefully — only pixels connected to your existing territory survive. Plan your drag path before activating.
📅Check the weekly theme — time your big purchases for weeks when your fandom category gets discounts.
⚔️Create or join a Clan — coordinate Faction Raids with clan members in Discord for maximum impact on the World War.
🏆Season Pass gives 2× XP and 50% more War Chest gold. Best investment if you play more than 3 times a week.
🎵TikTok your territory — click 🎵 TIKTOK in the right panel to copy a pre-formatted caption. Post it and bring your fandom to the war!
