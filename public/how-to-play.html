GETTING STARTED
⚡ QUICK START

Pixels of War is a real-time multiplayer territory game. Pick your fandom, buy pixels on the shared grid, and fight to keep your territory before the season ends. The fandom with the most pixels when the 90-day season timer hits zero wins the season and earns a permanent champion badge.

01

SELECT YOUR FANDOM

Browse 90+ fandoms across Gaming, Anime and Music. Search by name or filter by category. Click any fandom card to activate it — your color lights up on the grid.

02

DRAG TO CLAIM PIXELS

Click and drag on the canvas to select empty pixels. Release to claim them. Cost shows live in the action bar based on which sector you're in and how full it is. You must be in an unlocked sector.

03

DEFEND YOUR TERRITORY

Every claimed pixel gets a free 24-hour shield (cyan glow on canvas). After that it's vulnerable to raids. Use power-ups, Fortress shields, and Renewal Shields to protect what you've built.

04

WIN THE SEASON

The fandom holding the most pixels at season end is crowned champion and earns a permanent badge. The grid resets, a new season begins with a new theme, and the war starts over.

STRATEGY
💡 PRO TIPS
🎯

Claim center sectors first. They cost more but they're the most visible on the minimap and hardest to retake. Controlling the center dominates the leaderboard.

🛡️

Shield your most valuable territory. Auto-shield lasts 24h. After that use Fortress power-ups on your densest clusters — especially before going offline.

⚡

Wait for Sector Sale to expand. Prices halve during Sector Sale — the best time to claim expensive center pixels for half price.

♻️

Renew before pixels expire. Orange dots mean 30 days old — buy a Renewal Shield (€5) to reset your 50 oldest pixels before they hit 60 days and become claimable by anyone.

🔥

Log in every day. A 30-day streak earns 30 free pixels — €30 of territory for free. Free pixels are automatically used first before real money.

💣

Nuke dense unshielded clusters. A €50 Nuke destroys up to 400 pixels in a 20×20 zone. Most effective against enemies who haven't used Fortress.

🗺️

Use the minimap to navigate. Click anywhere on the minimap to jump to that location. The cyan rectangle shows your current viewport. Locked sectors appear dark on the minimap.
