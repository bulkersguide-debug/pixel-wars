HOW TO PLAY
CLAIM · RAID · DOMINATE

A 2000×2000 pixel grid. Hundreds of fandoms. One war. Claim territory for your fandom, raid your enemies, and survive until season end.

⚔️ START PLAYING →
⚡ THE BASICS
🏴
CLAIM
Pick a fandom. Drag on the grid to select empty pixels. Hit CLAIM and pay to lock them in. Your fandom's color fills the territory.
⚔️
RAID
Switch to RAID mode and drag over enemy pixels to steal them. Raids cost 2× the normal price. Shielded pixels cannot be raided.
👑
DOMINATE
The fandom with the most pixels at season end wins and enters the Hall of Fame permanently. Seasons last ~90 days.
🔐 GETTING STARTED — LOGIN
01
LOGIN WITH DISCORD
Click LOGIN WITH DISCORD in the header or on any locked action. We access your Discord username, user ID, avatar, and email address (used only to verify free pixel grants). No password required.
🎁
GET 25 FREE PIXELS
Every new player receives 25 free pixels automatically on first login. No purchase needed — use them to stake your first claim and start building territory right away.
🔒
GUEST MODE
You can view the grid and watch the war without logging in, but claiming pixels, raiding, chatting, alliances, and all other interactions require a Discord account.
📋 STEP BY STEP
01
PICK YOUR FANDOM
Browse the fandom list on the left panel. Search by name or filter by Gaming, Anime, or Music. Click a fandom to select it — your active fandom is shown in the bar below the canvas.
02
NAVIGATE THE GRID
Use the arrow pad to pan around the 2000×2000 grid. Click the minimap to jump to any location. The grid starts with the center sectors unlocked — more sectors open as the grid fills up.
03
SELECT PIXELS
In BUILD mode, click and drag to select empty pixels. The cost is calculated in real time and shown in the active bar. Bigger areas in busier sectors cost more.
04
CLAIM & PAY
Hit CLAIM to see the confirmation — pixels claimed, free pixels used, combo bonus if any, and final cost. Confirm to lock in your territory. Freshly claimed pixels get a 24h shield automatically.
05
DEFEND YOUR TERRITORY
Log in daily to check if your pixels are being raided. Enable push notifications and we'll alert you instantly when someone attacks. Use the Fortress power-up for extra protection.

🛡️ SHIELDS & DEFENCE
🔵24h Auto-Shield — every pixel you claim gets a 24-hour shield automatically. Shielded pixels show a cyan shimmer and cannot be raided.
🏰Fortress Power-Up — shields your last 30 pixels for 1 hour. Use it when you're under active attack.
♻️Renewal Shield — renews 50 of your oldest pixels, resetting their decay timer and adding a fresh shield.
⏳Pixel Decay — pixels older than 30 days start fading (yellow warning). After 60 days they expire and can be claimed by anyone. Log in regularly to renew your territory.

🤝 ALLIANCES & WARS
🤝
ALLIANCES
Propose an alliance with another fandom. If accepted, neither side can raid the other. Use it to coordinate against a common enemy — or betray them at the perfect moment for maximum drama.

Formally declare war on a fandom. It appears in every player's live feed as a big announcement, rallying both sides. Wars create the narrative that keeps fans engaged.
