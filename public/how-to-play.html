⚡ PRO TIPS
🗺️Build connected territory — adjacent claims cost 10% less. Expanding from your base is always cheaper than scattering pixels randomly.
🏰Avoid fortified sectors when raiding — 3× cost. Target edges of enemy territory in regular sectors instead.
💰Hold territory to earn gold — the more pixels you hold, the faster your War Chest grows. Don't spread too thin.
⚡Use Surge carefully — only pixels connected to your existing territory survive. Plan your expansion path before activating.
📅Check the weekly theme — time your big purchases for weeks when your fandom's category gets discounts.
🎲Use 🎲 RANDOM to jump to active battles — great for finding hot zones and watching wars unfold.
🕊️Peace Votes are only for top 3 fandoms. If you're leading and being raided hard, propose a ceasefire to let shields recharge.
🏆Season Pass gives 2× XP and 50% more War Chest gold. Best investment if you play more than 3 times a week.
