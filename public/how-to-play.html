01 What is Pixels of War?
A 4,000,000-pixel battlefield where fandoms fight for territory. Forever. Pixels of War is a real-money territory game. The grid is 2,000 × 2,000 pixels — every single pixel costs €1 and belongs permanently to whoever buys it. Once a pixel is claimed in your fandom's colour, it stays yours on the grid forever — unless someone raids you.

Pick your fandom — a game, anime, or music artist — and drag to claim territory. The more pixels your fandom owns, the higher it climbs the leaderboard. This isn't just buying a pixel. It's planting your flag and daring everyone else to take it.
02 How to Play — Step by Step
01

Choose your fandom

Scroll through the team browser at the bottom of the screen. Filter by Gaming 🎮, Anime 🎌, or Music 🎵 — then by genre. Click any fandom to select it. Your chosen fandom glows with its colour. This is your team for this session.

02

Navigate the grid

The grid is 2,000 × 2,000 pixels — you only see a section at a time. Use the arrow buttons to pan around the battlefield. Click anywhere on the minimap (top-right corner of the canvas) to instantly jump to any sector. The sector coordinates show in the bottom-left so you always know where you are.

03

Select your pixels

In BUILD mode, click and drag across the grid to select a rectangle of empty pixels. The selection glows in your fandom's colour. You can select as few as 1 pixel or as many as you want in one drag. The total cost appears in the claim bar at the top.

04

Pay and claim

Click CLAIM — a secure Stripe payment window opens. Enter your card details (or use Apple Pay / Google Pay). Payment goes through, pixels are permanently painted in your fandom's colour, and your territory grows on the live grid instantly. No refunds — pixels are forever.

05

Defend or raid

Switch to RAID mode ⚔️ to attack enemy territory. Select opponent pixels and pay €2 per pixel (double price) to steal them. Raided pixels flip to your fandom's colour permanently. Expect retaliation — rivals will see the live feed and strike back.

06

Use power-ups

Switch to SHOP mode 💥 to buy power-ups with instant destructive effects — drop a Cluster Bomb on enemy territory, unleash a Pixel Storm across the grid, or fire a precise Sniper shot. Power-ups can change the war in seconds.

08 Navigating the Grid
The grid is massive. Here's how to move around it. The full 2,000×2,000 battlefield has 20×20 named sectors — from Sector 1-1 in the top-left to Sector 20-20 in the bottom-right. Your current sector shows in the bottom-left of the canvas at all times.

Minimap — the small 200×200 overview in the top-right corner of the canvas shows the entire grid. Every claimed pixel appears in its fandom's colour. The cyan rectangle shows your current viewport. Click anywhere on the minimap to instantly jump to that area.

Arrow buttons — the 8-directional arrow pad below the canvas pans your view 50–100 grid cells at a time. Use diagonals (↖ ↗ ↙ ↘) to move faster across the map.

Pro tip: Study the minimap before raiding. Find isolated enemy territories surrounded by empty space — they're the easiest to cut off and overwhelm.
09 Pro Strategy Tips
🗺️

Claim connected territory

Don't scatter pixels randomly. Build solid connected blocks — they're much harder to raid and split. A 10×10 square is far stronger than 100 isolated pixels spread across the map.

⏰

Watch for battle events

The event banner at the top is your best friend. During Chaos Hour, raiding costs half price — save your raid budget and unleash it all when the event fires. During Mega Bonus, buy your biggest purchases for maximum free pixels.

💣

Use the Nuke strategically

At €50 the Nuke is the most powerful weapon in the game — 20×20 = up to 400 enemy pixels wiped at once. Use it on the leaderboard leader to close the gap fast. It fires anywhere on the full 2,000×2,000 grid.

🔥

Trigger combos for free pixels

Always try to select 15+ pixels in a single drag. You get 30% bonus pixels completely free. On a 50-pixel purchase that's 15 free pixels — essentially a €15 discount. Never buy fewer than 10 at a time.

📡

Watch the live feed

The feed on the right shows every move happening in real time — who's raiding who, who just dropped a bomb, which fandom is surging. If your fandom appears in the feed as a raid target, respond immediately before they take too much.

🤝

Organise your fandom

The biggest wins come from coordination. Share the minimap screenshot in your fan community — Discord, Reddit, group chats. Agree on a sector to focus on. Ten fans each buying 20 pixels in the same area creates an unbreakable fortress.

10 Rules & Important Info
💳 Payments are real. Every pixel costs real money. Transactions are processed securely by Stripe. We never store your card details.

🔒 Pixels are permanent. Once a pixel is purchased it belongs to your fandom permanently — unless someone raids it. No refunds are issued for purchased pixels.

⚔️ Raids are part of the game. Having your pixels raided is not a bug — it's the core mechanic. If your territory gets hit, fight back. That's the war.

🌍 One grid, all players. Every player in the world sees the same live grid. There are no separate servers or instances. When you claim a pixel, the whole world sees it instantly.

🏆 Seasons reset. Every 90 days the grid resets for a new season. Winners from the previous season receive a permanent badge. The war starts fresh — and the battle for territory begins again.
