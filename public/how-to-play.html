01 What is Pixels of War?
A 4,000,000-pixel battlefield where fandoms fight for territory. Forever. Pixels of War is a real-money territory game. The grid is 2,000 × 2,000 pixels — every pixel costs €1 and belongs permanently to whoever buys it. Claimed pixels are painted in your fandom's colour on a shared live grid that every player in the world sees in real time.

But here's the twist — enemies can raid your territory. Pixels can be stolen unless you defend them. That's where shields, power-ups, and daily rewards come in. You can also earn free pixels every day just by logging in — no payment required to participate.
02 How to Play — Step by Step
01

Claim your daily reward first

Every time you visit for the first time that day, a Daily Reward popup appears automatically. Tap CLAIM to collect free pixels based on your streak. Your first day gives 1 free pixel — by day 7 you earn 10 free pixels. You can only claim once per day — after claiming the badge in the header shows ✅ CLAIMED and tapping it again shows a reminder toast instead of giving more pixels. Free pixels add to your balance shown in the header.

02

Choose your fandom

Scroll through the team browser at the bottom. Filter by Gaming 🎮, Anime 🎌, or Music 🎵 — then by genre. Click any fandom to select it. Your fandom glows in its colour. This is your team for the battle.

03

Navigate the grid

The grid is 2,000 × 2,000 pixels — you only see a section at a time. Use the arrow buttons to pan in 8 directions. Click anywhere on the minimap (top-right of the canvas) to jump to that zone instantly. Your sector coordinates show in the bottom-left corner.

04

Select and claim pixels

In BUILD mode, click and drag across empty pixels to select a rectangle. The CLAIM bar shows your cost — if you have free pixels from your daily streak, they're used automatically first (shown as 🎁3FREE + €7). Click CLAIM to pay and plant your flag. All newly claimed pixels are automatically shielded for 24 hours — they glow cyan on the grid.

05

Raid enemy territory

Switch to RAID mode ⚔️ to attack. Select enemy pixels and pay €2 per pixel to conquer them. Important: you cannot raid pixels that are shielded (glowing cyan). Wait for the shield to expire, or use a power-up to blast through unshielded zones. Raids appear in the live feed — expect retaliation.

06

Defend your territory

Your pixels are automatically protected for 24 hours after every purchase. To extend protection, buy the 🛡️ Fortress power-up (€8) which shields your last 30 pixels for 1 full hour. Shielded pixels show a cyan glow on the grid and cannot be raided, bombed, or nuked by anyone.

07

Use power-ups to change the war

Switch to SHOP mode 💥 to buy devastating weapons — drop a Cluster Bomb on enemy territory, nuke a 20×20 zone, or airdrop a 15×15 claim anywhere on the map. All power-up claims also come with 24-hour shields on the pixels they capture.

04 The Shield System — How Defence Works
⏱️ AUTO · FREE · 24 HOURS

24-Hour Grace Period

Every pixel you buy — whether through BUILD mode, power-ups, or daily rewards — is automatically shielded for 24 hours. No action required. Shielded pixels glow cyan on the grid. After 24 hours the protection expires and the pixel can be raided.

🏰 POWER-UP · €8 · 1 HOUR

Fortress Shield

Activating the Fortress power-up shields your last 30 purchased pixels for 1 full hour. Use it when you see a raid incoming in the live feed. Fortress shields stack on top of the 24h grace — your pixels can be double-shielded.

🗺️ VISUAL · ON CANVAS + MINIMAP

Spotting Shields

Shielded pixels glow cyan on the main canvas with a subtle dot pattern. On the minimap, cyan specks show protected zones across the full 2000×2000 grid. Use the minimap to find unprotected enemy territory before raiding.

Important: Bombs, Nukes, and Snipers all skip shielded pixels — they only destroy or steal unprotected ones. This means even the most expensive weapons cannot crack a well-shielded position. Timing your Fortress activations is a key part of winning the war.

10 Pro Strategy Tips
🛡️

Time your Fortress activations

Don't waste a Fortress the moment you buy pixels — they already have 24h grace. Save it for when you see in the live feed that someone is actively raiding your fandom. Activate Fortress then and their attacks bounce off.

🗺️

Use the minimap to find unshielded targets

Before raiding, open the minimap and look for enemy territory that has no cyan glow. Those are unprotected pixels from older purchases — much cheaper to raid than fresh cyan ones. Strike there first.

🎁

Never miss your daily check-in

The free pixel balance adds up fast. A 30-day streak gives you 30 free pixels in a single day plus the Month Champion badge. Even on days you don't want to play, just open the site, claim your reward, and leave. It takes 10 seconds.

🔥

Always buy 15+ pixels at a time

The 30% combo bonus is the best deal in the game. On a €15 purchase you get 19-20 pixels instead of 15. Over time this compounds — players who always combo-buy end up with 30% more territory for the same money.

⚡

Spend free pixels during Chaos Hour

When Chaos Hour fires, raids cost 50% less. If you've been saving free pixels from your daily streak, deploy them NOW — grab territory while it's cheap to defend and expensive for others to take back.

🤝

Organise your fandom

Share the minimap screenshot in your fan Discord or group chat. Agree on a sector. Ten fans each buying 20 pixels in one area creates a shielded fortress that's economically impossible to raid for 24 hours. Coordination always beats solo play.
